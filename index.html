Mr. Swapon Kanti Dey

Council of Ministers Secretary

Mr. Swapon Kanti Dey joined the Cabinet Department as Cabinet Secretary on 03 January 2023.

Before joining this department, he served as Senior Secretary of Energy and Mineral Resources Department from 02 January 2022 to 02 January 2023 and as Secretary of Secondary and Higher Education Department from 31 December 2019 to 01 January 2022.
